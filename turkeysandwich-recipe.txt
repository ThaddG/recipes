Ingredients:
-2 slices of bread
-turkey
-avocado
-onions
-tomatoes
-mustard

Directions:
1) make a stripped down guac by mashing avocado and sprinkling salt & pepper
   and a drop of lime (optional)
2) spread the avocado and mustard on both slices of bread
3) on one slice add tomatoes, turkey, then onions
4) put together and enjoy
